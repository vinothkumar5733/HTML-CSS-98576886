Instruction for the Softcopy of Students Photograph

Softcopy of Photograph: Softcopy of passport size photo must be submitted to class coordinator along with certificates. Specific Requirements:

  • Size: 2 inches x 2 inches (51 mm x 51 mm), Not to exceed 100 KB and not less than 20 KB.
  • DPI: Between 200 and 300.
  • Background: Plain white or off-white.
  • Expression: Neutral, eyes open, and looking directly at the camera.
  • Face: Full face, front view, from the top of the head to the bottom of the chin.
  • Head Position: Centered within the frame.
  • Lighting: Uniform, no shadows or red-eye.
  • Hair: No hair obscuring the face.
  • Eyeglasses: If worn, should be clear and thin-framed, with no flash reflection or obscured eyes.
  • Head Coverings: Only allowed for religious reasons, with face clearly visible from bottom of chin to top of forehead.
  • Recentness: Photos should be recent, preferably taken within the last six months.
  • Other People/Objects: None visible in the photo.

⚠️ Note: No selfie photo is allowed.
